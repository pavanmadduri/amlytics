About Us

A boutique built on Hyderabadi tradition.

Welcome to Elite Designer Boutique, located right next to Chaitanyapuri Metro at PVT Mall, Shop No. 350. For over a decade, Vijaya garu has been Dilsukhnagar's go-to designer for women who want to look stunning without breaking the bank.
