About Amlytics

A focused consulting practice — built on clarity, rigour, and results.

Amlytics was founded on a simple conviction: most organisations don't lack ideas — they lack the clarity and discipline to act on them.

Operated as a sole proprietorship and led personally by its founder, Amlytics offers consulting services that pair analytical rigour with commercial pragmatism. Every engagement is scoped, delivered, and reviewed by the founder directly — no junior hand-offs, no recycled templates.

With over four years of hands-on consulting experience across growing businesses, the practice has developed a working style that clients consistently describe as fast, honest, and commercially sharp.

How we work

A disciplined four-step engagement model.

No mystery boxes and no open-ended retainers. Every engagement follows the same rhythm — calibrated to the problem's shape and the client's tempo.

STEP 01

Discover

A structured discovery call followed by a fixed-fee diagnostic — so both sides understand the problem before committing to the work.

STEP 02

Scope

A written scope of work with deliverables, timelines, and a fixed investment — no hourly billing, no scope creep, no surprises.

STEP 03

Deliver

Weekly working sessions with the founder directly. Deliverables are reviewed in real time — not handed over only at the end.

STEP 04

Transition

A deliberate handover with an optional 30-day advisory runway — so your team carries the work forward with confidence.
